# Contrail

Contrail is an open sourced flight simulator that features servers of up to 60 players in a map inspired by the harsh conditions of Alaska. Fly around mountain ranges, over rivers, along the coast. And stop at a variety of locations from detailed airstrips to full airports.

The game prioritizes bush flying and small aircraft in harsh weather to prevent boring hour long flights driven by autopilot. Strong winds may destabilize your aircraft and you will have to plan against weather conditions for your trips.

The game also features a player driven economy and licensing system allowing players to natuarally progress from small bush craft to larger twin prop aircraft. Fuel costs are real and you have to service your plane to prevent failures.

The player earns money by doing cargo hauls, charter flights, or by reselling aircraft purchared from other players. You can also start airlines with your friends.
